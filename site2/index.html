About Craftsman

A family-owned shutter manufacturer operating in Scarborough, Ontario since 1995, Craftsman Custom Built Shutters has twice outgrown its manufacturing facilities. Starting with only 3 crew members we built the company from the ground up. Today, we manufacture our shutters and manage operations at our head office in Scarborough. After working in the industry for more than 17 years assisting other shutter manufacturing companies in establishing and growing their businesses, we decided to draw on our combined knowledge and experience to produce an even higher quality shutter.
